Software Development

I've always wanted to work in technology; as a kid, I saw myself creating fun apps and websites for my friends and myself to use. Since I was a tiny child, I've had a fascination with technology. Playing Kirby, Metroid, Mario and Luigi: SuperStar Saga on my hand-me-down Gameboy, I have always wanted to know how and why it worked. This fantization stuck with me when I switched to Windows computers, as I grew older, I still wanted to learn how and why. I want to learn computer languages, and this year I'm finally pursuing that goal. Software development was one of the jobs that caught my attention. Software developers create designs and build computer systems. While others create new mobile or desktop applications.

The Institutions that I will apply to would be UC Berkeley, University of Texas at Austin, University of Southern California, or UC Davis as the institutions where I could finish my bachelor's degree. All of those universities have excellent to solid programs in Software Development and other technology-related fields. In those Institutions, I'll major in Computer Science or Engineering and learn more coding languages like Python, C++, C#, and Java. I am ambitious and tenacious, with a good GPA and have done a lot of AP classes and tests. I'm also a poor minority, a first generation Mexican-American, with a good story. So, maybe, one of them will accept me. However,if not, I'm fine with applying to other institutions and universities,it makes no difference to me which institutions I attend as long as I receive the education I desire.

Paying for college/university will be tough for me because my family is poor. However, I'll take advantage of any resources available to me, such as student aid, and I'll use my savings from working. Which means I'll have to save even more money, so I'll less spend money on games, clothing and other recrational items. Hopefully, one of the colleges to which I applied will provide me a scholarship but if not ill make due somehow.
